Difference between localstorage VS session storage

After assigning any key/values in LOCALSTORAGE when we close the tab and again open the tab or open a new window tab, the assigning keys/values will still exist. In contrast, the allocating keys/values will be removed after closing the window. That's mean SESSIONSTORAGE is temporary storage.
